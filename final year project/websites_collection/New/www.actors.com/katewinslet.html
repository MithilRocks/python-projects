Kate Winslet

Kate Winslet was born in Reading, Berkshire, England, UK on October 5, 1975. She is well-known for films like Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind (2004) and Titanic (1997). She was nominated for the Oscars for her performances in Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind (2004), Titanic (1997) but won for The Reader (2008).
